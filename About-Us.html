Meet the Developer

I'm Aarav, the founder of Mind Mirths, a platform aimed at making therapy services more accessible for everyone. At a young age, I decided to take on the important issue of mental health awareness.

After winning a hackathon called Mindful Hacks 2023, I didn’t stop there. I continued improving the platform, reaching out to clinics, and refining the website to help bring affordable healthcare closer to reality.

Currently, I’m leading this initiative as part of The Impart Accelerator Programme, which focuses on supporting youth-led projects centered around mental wellness and awareness. The Accelerator Program funded $2,300 towards my ​initiative and I am proud to say we are supporting people day by day!

Our target group includes teams working on solutions to address mental health challenges faced by youth aged 10 and above in Singapore.

I’m passionate about making a difference, and I look forward to sharing more exciting updates soon!
